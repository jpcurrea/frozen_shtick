## Key Items & Afflictions

### Blue Vials
**Type**: Magical/alchemical items from Eiselcross  
**Origin**: Brought back by Urgon Wenth from Eiselcross
**Original Count**: At least 2 vials (brought by Urgon)
**Status**: Stolen by Tulgi Latan approximately one month before Session 1  
**Previous Location**: Pelc's Curiosities (after Urgon's death)  
**Current Location**: Unknown (were in Croker's Cave, current whereabouts uncertain)  
**Danger**: Contact causes Frigid Woe disease

**Timeline**:
1. Urgon Wenth brings vials back from Eiselcross
2. Stored at Pelc's Curiosities (possibly among Urgon's possessions)
3. Tulgi Latan robs Pelc's Curiosities ~1 month before Session 1
4. Tulgi contracts Frigid Woe after the robbery
5. Vials end up in Croker's Cave (Hulil's hideout)
6. Sick individuals in cave contract disease from vial contact

**Mysteries**:
- Original creator unknown
- Intended purpose unknown
- How many total vials exist?
- Are they weapons or were they being transported for another purpose?
- Why did Urgon bring them back from Eiselcross?

### Frigid Woe (Disease)
**Type**: Magical disease/curse  
**Transmission**: Contact with blue vials from Eiselcross  
**Source**: Blue vials that Urgon Wenth brought back from Eiselcross

**Symptoms** (Based on Urgon Wenth's progression):
1. Veins begin turning blue
2. Movement progressively slows
3. Over the course of several weeks, victim transforms into an ice statue
4. Death (complete transformation into ice)

**Known Victims**:
- **Urgon Wenth**: Deceased (turned into ice statue before Session 1)
- **Tulgi Latan**: Currently infected (contracted after robbing Pelc's Curiosities)
- **At least 1 individual in Croker's Cave**: Confirmed infected in Session 3
- **At least 1 other sick individual in Croker's Cave**: Status unclear

**Timeline**: Disease progresses over several weeks from initial contact to death  
**Cure**: Unknown (party quest to find)  
**Connection**: Central to the "Frozen Sick" adventure

## Campaign Notes

### "Frozen Sick" Mini-Adventure
- **Setting**: Palebank Village and surrounding areas
- **Arakki's Starting Point**: Working as laborer, hiding her identity
- **Central Mystery**: Frigid Woe disease and stolen blue vials

### Party Dynamics
- **Arakki**: Recent arrival to Palebank Village, hiding her divine heritage and fire powers
- **Jwala**: Living in village for 6+ months, chaotic neutral dragonborn with cut-off wings
- **Serelion**: Living in village for 6+ months, neutral good high elf
- **Party Commitment**: Hired by Elro Aldataur for 100 gold to find the cause of Frigid Woe

### Session Summaries

#### Session 1: Urgon's Funeral & The Investigation Begins
**Location**: Palebank Village, Urgon Wenth's home  
**Key Events**:
- Party attended the funeral of **Urgon Wenth**, who died from Frigid Woe
- Witnessed the symptoms: blue veins, slowed movement, eventual transformation into ice statue over several weeks
- Met **Elro Aldataur**, village chief, who hired the party to track down the cause of the affliction
- **Contract**: 100 gold to find the cause of Frigid Woe
- Investigated Urgon's home while **Mila**, a village guard, kept watch
- **Serelion and Mila**: Previously acquainted, showed mutual interest during the encounter
- **Jwala's Deception**: Lied to Mila about intentions to steal, then lied about having stolen items from Urgon's home
- **Mila's Insight**: Recognized Jwala's lying tell immediately, warned party not to mention stolen items or she'd be in trouble

**Key Information Learned**:
- Urgon had brought back items from **Eiselcross**, including blue vials
- The vials are connected to Frigid Woe

**NPCs Introduced**:
- Elro Aldataur (village chief)
- Mila (village guard)
- Urgon Wenth (deceased victim)

---

#### Session 2: Pelc's Curiosities Ambush
**Location**: Pelc's Curiosities (shop in Palebank Village)  
**Key Events**:
- Party arrived at Pelc's Curiosities to find **5 elf bandits** ransacking the place
- **Combat Encounter**: Swift and decisive victory for the party
  - **Arakki's Fire Bolt**: Punched a hole through one bandit's chest, killing instantly
  - **Serelion's Rapid Strike**: Struck 3 bandits at once with devastating effect
  - 4 bandits killed, 1 survivor captured
- **Interrogation**: Last surviving bandit revealed crucial information

**Key Information Learned**:
- **Hulil Latan**: Sister of Tulgi Latan, hiding at **Croaker Cave** with her bandit team
- **Tulgi Latan**: Robbed Pelc's Curiosities approximately one month before Session 1
- Among stolen items: **Two blue vials** that Urgon Wenth had brought back from Eiselcross
- **Tulgi's Condition**: Came down with Frigid Woe shortly after the robbery
- **Connection Established**: Blue vials from Eiselcross → Robbery → Frigid Woe infection

**NPCs Introduced**:
- Hulil Latan (mentioned, in hiding at Croaker Cave)
- Tulgi Latan (mentioned, infected with Frigid Woe)
- Pelc's Curiosities elf bandits (5 total, 4 deceased)

**Next Step**: Party now knows to investigate Croaker Cave to find Hulil's bandit hideout

---

#### Session 3: Croker's Cave Adventure
**Location**: Croker's Cave
**Key Events**:
- Party explored Croker's Cave
- Combat encounter with giant frogs (party member was grappled in water)
- Discovered bandits occupying the cave (non-hostile encounter)
- Bandits guided party deeper into cave system
- Found a campsite with 10 roll-out beds (party initially mistook for a ritual circle)
- Discovered hidden trap hole under one of the beds (fortunately no one fell in)
- **Major Discovery**: In the deepest chamber, found **Hulil Latan** (infected with Frigid Woe) and her assistant (not sick)
- **Hulil named the affliction**: She called it "Frigid Woe" - first time the party learned its name
- Learned Hulil contracted the disease through contact with the stolen blue vials
- Found mysterious **dragon-faced chest** beside Hulil's bed

**Key Items Found**:
- **Bald Dwarf Whiskey**: Bottle found at the campsite (taken by Serelion)
- **Dragon-Faced Chest**: Locked chest with dragon carving, beside Hulil's bed
- **Blue Vials**: Mysterious stolen items that cause Frigid Woe when contacted (referenced, not found)

**NPCs Encountered**:
- **Hulil Latan**: Found sick with Frigid Woe in deepest chamber
- **Hulil's Assistant**: Healthy, attending to Hulil
- **Croker's Cave Bandits**: Non-hostile, guided party deeper

**Active Quest**: Find a cure for Frigid Woe

**Mysteries to Investigate** *(Player-Safe)*:

- What is the intended purpose of the blue vials? (Weapons? Transport cargo? Something else?)
- Are there more blue vials beyond the two Urgon brought back?
- Where are the stolen vials now? (Were in Croker's Cave, current location unknown)
- Are there more infected individuals beyond those already found?
- Who set up the campsite with 10 beds in Croker's Cave?
- What was the trap hole intended to catch/protect?
- How is Hulil Latan connected to the larger mystery?
- What is inside the dragon-faced chest beside Hulil's bed?
- What is happening in Eiselcross that's producing these dangerous vials?
